Originally founded in 2008, Viking Robotics is a FIRST (For Inspiration & Recognition of Science & Technology) Robotics team. We are located at Ballard High School in Seattle, Washington and are now over 50 members strong.
We participate in several FIRST Robotics competition events every year and work to spread information about FIRST and its programs through our community. The benefits of a STEM (Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics) robotics team include problem solving, teamwork, time management, and life skills.
Our alumni now attend schools such as Gonzaga University, Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, University of Colorado Denver, California Polytechnic State University, University of Washington, Washington State University, and Western Washington University.
